Do you have those gnomes in your garden? Make them do something useful, like gathering data about your soil humidity and report it to you via email when it's time to watter. With such garden sentinels you will never forget to watter in time anymore.

We plan to build different types of gnomes. Each will come with different sensors. There are gnomes which can measure soil humidity, relative air humidity, temperature and pressure. There are also big bosses which cam sense many parameters at once.

FAQ

What is iGnom?
iGnom is an ordinary garden gnome with sensors on-board. It can sense environment parameters and send data over internet. It can notify you about drained soil in your garden or at home.
Which environment data can iGnom gather?
Right now we have working prototype with soil-humidity sensors, but we plan to add more sensors (light, temperature, relative air humidity, pressure) if things go well.
